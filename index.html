01 — About Sonny

From the Courts of Kentucky
to the Philippines

Liborio "Sonny" Albano is not your average sports instructor. With over four decades of competitive tennis under his belt and a nationally ranked standing in Senior Olympic USA, Sonny has spent a lifetime mastering the art of racket sports — and teaching others to love them just as much.

As the former Tennis Pro at Elizabethtown Country Club in Elizabeth, Kentucky, Sonny honed his coaching craft at every level — high school, college, and professional clubs. His patient, diagnostic approach to teaching has helped hundreds of students correct technique, build confidence, and fall in love with the game.

Now based in Nashville, TN with deep roots in the Philippines, Sonny is channeling his passion into a new mission: bringing world-class pickleball and tennis instruction to Filipino communities, helping young kids, adults, and seniors stay active, competitive, and joyful on the court.

02 — Background

A Career Built
on the Court

Age 12

Picked Up a Racket

Sonny's love affair with tennis began at age 12 — a passion that would grow into a 40+ year competitive career spanning leagues, tournaments, and national championships.

Pro Era

Tennis Pro — Elizabethtown Country Club

Served as resident Tennis Professional at Elizabethtown Country Club in Elizabeth, Kentucky — coaching youth, high school teams, college players, and club members at every skill level.

National

Ranked #4 — Senior Tennis Olympic USA

Competing at the highest level of senior tennis, Sonny earned a national ranking of #4 representing the state of Tennessee — a testament to decades of dedication and elite-level play.

2022

Fort Lauderdale Senior Olympics

Competed at the national Fort Lauderdale Senior Olympic Games, finishing 4th place in tennis — continuing his elite competitive journey well into his senior years.

Pickle

Mastering Pickleball

After 7 years of dedicated play, Sonny took his first Major — winning 1st Place at the Tennessee Senior Games in Pickleball. His tennis foundation makes him a formidable force on the pickleball court.

Now

Coming to the Philippines 🇵🇭

Sonny is bringing his lifetime of expertise to the Philippines — offering premium pickleball and tennis instruction to youth, adults, and seniors who want to learn, compete, and stay active.
